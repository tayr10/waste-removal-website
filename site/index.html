Important — Standard Operating Condition
Regarding Mill Rights

Mill rights is not a service we offer — it is the condition under which we operate. All materials, scrap metal, and salvageable items removed during any job become the property of Ironclad Recycling LLC. This applies across all services: cleanouts, demolition, rigging, and scrap removal.

The value we recover from recycled materials is factored into the overall cost of each job. If you have questions about how mill rights apply to your specific project, please call us before booking: 508-769-2282.
